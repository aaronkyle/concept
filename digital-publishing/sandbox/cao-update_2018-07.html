CAO's latest newsletter provides an update on progress made through dispute resolution in Cambodia and Morocco, the launch of an animated video explaining CAO's work, the release of a compliance investigation related to a paper project in Malaysia, new CAO advisory supply chain work, and outreach events in the Africa region.  Read more below. 

Company and Community Representatives Conduct Joint Land Demarcation in Cambodia
Two joint land demarcation missions were recently carried out in Ratanakiri province, Cambodia as part of an ongoing CAO dispute resolution process between 12 affected villages and a Vietnamese rubber company.

IFC Commits to Undertake Remedial Steps in Bujagali Dam Case in Uganda
In May 2018, IFC released an addendum to its management response concerning worker and land issues raised in CAO's investigations related to the Bujagali Dam project in Uganda.
Learn more

 

CAO Releases Investigation Report on IFC's Bilt Paper Project in Malaysia
In June 2018, CAO released an investigation related to IFC’s  investment in Bilt Paper, a pulp and paper project in Malaysia.
Learn more

CAO Initiates Compliance Investigation of IFC's Alto Maipo Hydropower Project in Chile
In May 2018, CAO released an appraisal report determining that an investigation of IFC's investment in the Alto Maipo project in Chile is merited.  
Learn more

 

CAO Launches Advisory Memos on Supply Chain 
CAO has released new advisory work on Supply Chain Business Opportunities and Risks.  CAO developed advice on this topic based on lessons from CAO cases and inputs from external experts and IFC staff.

CAO Event: Raising the Bar for Financial Sector Transparency
