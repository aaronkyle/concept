HOW TO FILE A COMPLAINT

Select Language:   

CAO

CAO is the independent accountability mechanism for projects supported by the private sector arms of the World Bank Group - the International Finance Corporation (IFC) and Multilateral Investment Guarantee Agency (MIGA).

CAO has 3 simple eligibility criteria for a complaint to qualify for assessment:


01

Complaint relates to an IFC or MIGA project (including projects under consideration)




02

Complaint relates to social and/or environmental issues associated with that project(s)




03

Complainant believes they are, or may be, affected by the social and/or environmental issues raised


FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

  • Any individual, group, community, or other party can make a complaint to CAO if they believe they are, or may be, affected by an IFC or MIGA project(s). Complaints may be made on behalf of those affected by a representative or organization.
